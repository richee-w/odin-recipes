This is Lasagna 3

Lasagna is delicious and even better with this recipe. See the ingredients below, mix it up, cook it and eat, lovely!

Ingredients

  • Flat Pasta
  • Minced Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Puree
  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Chop onions
  3. Mix up beef and onions
  4. Add tomatoes
  5. Add tomato puree
  6. Do a layer of pasta
  7. Do a layer of meat and onions
  8. Repeat until used up ingredients
  9. Put in over for 2 hours
  10. Watch a movie
  11. Serve and eat
