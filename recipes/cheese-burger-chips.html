Cheese Burger and Chips

Cheese Burger and chips for lazy people

I don't like to spend too much time in the kitchen, so let's keep it to a minimum.

Ingredients

  • Frozen burgers
  • Cheese slices
  • Oven chips
  • Burger bun
  • Tomato ketchup and/or Mustard or not

Method

  1. Follow instructions on the frozen burger packaging to cook the burgers
  2. Do the same as in step 1 for the chips
  3. Set the timer on the oven, watch a movie on Netflix
  4. When the timer goes off, pause the movie on Netflix
  5. Place a slice of cheese in the Burger bun
  6. Place the burger in the Burger bun
  7. Add Ketchup and Mustard to taste or not
  8. Unpause movie on Netflix, enjoy your food and watch the movie
