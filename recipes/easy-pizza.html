Easy Pizza

This Pizza recipe is the easiest yet

This is so easy that you will hardly have to move off your sofa. You'll only have to pause your Netflix movie for a minute or two.

Ingredients

  • Mobile phone with internet access
  • Some money

Method

  1. Search for a local Pizza delivery establishment on your mobile phone.
  2. Check the menu discovered as a result of completing step 1.
  3. Choose your favorite Pizza as discovered in step 2.
  4. Call the Pizza company on your mobile phone and order the Pizza you discovered in step 3.
  5. Start watching a Netflix movie.
  6. After about half an hour, listen for the Pizza delivery man at your door. You may have to turn down the volume on your movie.
  7. Answer the door when Pizza delivery turns up. Don't forget to pause the Netflix movie.
  8. Pay the man and remember to leave a tip.
  9. Unpause Netflix, enjoy the Pizza and the movie.
